What if we told you that the creator may have based the characters from the Hundred Acre Wood on various mental illnesses? Pooh with ADHD, Piglet wih Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Eeyore with Depression, Rabbit with Narcissism, Owl with Dyslexia, and Tigger with Extreme Risk Taking Behaviors. And despite their troubles, the characters always had the support of one another. Our team sought out to create an online community that encompasses much of how the Hundred Acre Wood felt to us as Children: a community for emotional support for all users.
